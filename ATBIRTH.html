    Hepatitis B birth dose

    This is a single dose vaccine. Administered via injection This vaccine protects against Hepatitis B which is a viral infection that attacks the liver and can cause both acute and chronic disease.

    Potential side effects of this vaccine include:

    Other than some redness and soreness where the injection was given, side effects are rare. It's an inactivated (dead) vaccine, so it cannot cause the infection itself.
