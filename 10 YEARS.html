    Tetanus-TT

    The tetanus shots are safe and effective at preventing tetanus. Vaccines like any medicine, can have side effects. These are usually mild and go away on their own.

    1.Protects your child from tetanus, which can be a serious disease, as well as diphtheria and whooping cough (pertussis).

    2.Protects your child from painful muscle stiffness from tetanus.

    3.Keeps your child from missing school or child care, and you from missing work.
